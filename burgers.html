Burgers

Description

A burger is a food item consisting of a cooked patty, usually ground meat (most commonly beef), placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. It is often served with various toppings and condiments.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Meat
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Spread sauce on the buns
  2. Grill the meat perfectly
  3. Place the meat between the buns
  4. Add vegetables and pickles
