Kabsa

Description

Kabsa is a traditional Saudi Arabian rice dish, considered the national dish of the country. It's a flavorful and aromatic meal made with rice, meat (often chicken or lamb), and a blend of spices.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Rice
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Add tomato sauce to water
  2. Add rice to the mixture
  3. Place the cooked chicken with the rice
  4. Add vegetables and almonds and mix ingredients until done
