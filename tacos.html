Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla, typically corn or wheat, filled with a variety of ingredients

Ingredients

  • tacos
  • Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Meat
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Spread sauce on the tacos
  2. Grill the meat perfectly
  3. Place the meat in the tacos
  4. Add vegetables and pickles
